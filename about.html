About Us

At Chubby Foods, we believe that food is more than just a meal – it’s an experience. Located in Meegoda and at Winners Institute Cafe, we bring homemade goodness to your table, offering a wide variety of delicious meals crafted with love and care. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or just looking for a satisfying meal, we’ve got something for everyone. We pride ourselves on using the freshest ingredients, creating mouthwatering dishes that are perfect for any occasion. From intimate family gatherings to large events, our meals are designed to bring people together and create lasting memories. Ordering with us is easy! You can place your orders through Facebook or WhatsApp, and we'll deliver your delicious meals right to your doorstep. Whether you’re near our food shop at Puwakwaththa Road or at Winners Institute Cafe, we’re always ready to serve you with a smile.
